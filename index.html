My Story

Placing creativity and human interaction at the center of his career, Alvaro has focused the last 5 years of his work life on helping companies grow their influence in Latin America through effective marketing campaigns that require managing groups of people as well as the usage of a variety of skills such as public speaking, strategizing, content creation and most importantly, effective communication.

Being a Songwriter, Alvaro is very familiar with sharing his vision and surrounding himself with talented people to make his vision go from an idea to a reality. His passion for music lead him to learn a variety of instruments such as drums, guitar, bass, and piano, as well as become proficient in software such as Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro X, Gimp, and Google Docs.

Alvaro is currently interested in tech startups that are helping to make services accessible to everyone and fundamentally make the world a better place.
